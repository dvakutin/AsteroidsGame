Somewhere in the Galaxy: Asteroids Game

Press "5" to accelerate spaceship
Press "6" to turn spaceship right
Press "4" to turn spaceship left
Press "h" for hyperspace
If the spaceship collides with an asteroid: -5 health points
If the spaceship shoots an asteroid: +1 health point
If your health score reaches zero, game over :(
Good luck and try to win by shooting all the asteroids!
